Welcome to PSU 3D Printing Club

A Club Dedicated to 3D Printing and what it can do

Hello! The Penn State 3-D Printing Club provides students with the means and opportunity to construct and work with open source 3-D printers and projects associated with 3-D printers. Come join us for our weekly meetings Sundays at 6 in 312 Hammond!

For those of you interested in using the 3-D Printing Club for your classes or small business ventures, feel free to click the link below or the picture above and fill out the form to submit what you would like printed. We are happy to make your dreams a reality!
